Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my DefenseSphere software?

To update your DefenseSphere software, open the application and navigate to Settings > Updates. Click on "Check for Updates" and follow the prompts to install the latest version.

What should I do if I suspect my account has been compromised?

If you suspect your account has been compromised, immediately change your password, enable two-factor authentication if not already active, and contact our support team for a security review.

How can I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, log in to your account, go to Billing & Subscriptions, and click on "Cancel Subscription." Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process.
